Chicken Curry

Ingredients: Chicken, onions, tomatoes, garlic, ginger, turmeric, cumin, coriander, garam masala, chili powder.

Instructions: Saute onions, garlic, and ginger. Add spices and chicken. Cook until tender. Add tomatoes and simmer.

Beef Curry

Ingredients: Beef, onions, garlic, ginger, tomatoes, yogurt, cumin, coriander, turmeric, garam masala.

Instructions: Brown beef, saute onions, garlic, and ginger. Add spices and yogurt. Simmer until beef is tender.

Sushi

Ingredients: Sushi rice, nori seaweed, fresh fish (salmon/tuna), soy sauce, wasabi, rice vinegar.

Instructions: Cook and season rice. Place on nori, add fish, and roll tightly. Slice and serve with soy sauce and wasabi.
